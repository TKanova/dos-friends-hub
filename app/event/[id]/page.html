About This Event

Join us for the annual Campus Music Festival featuring local student bands, food trucks, and amazing performances. This year's lineup includes indie rock, electronic, and acoustic acts from across the region. Don't miss this incredible night of music and community!

The festival will feature multiple stages, food vendors, and activities for all ages. Bring your friends, family, and dancing shoes for an unforgettable evening under the stars.

Free admission for all students with valid ID. General admission tickets available at the door for $10.
